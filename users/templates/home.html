Welcome to the Homework Tracker

A fun and easy way for teachers and pupils to stay on top of homework with a surprise at the end!

What is Homework Tracker?

Homework Tracker is an online platform designed to help teachers manage homework for their classes, and motivate pupils with a playful reward system.

How it works for Teachers
  • Register online as a teacher
  • Create child accounts for your pupils
  • Set and assign homework tasks to your class
How it works for Pupils
  • Login with the details your teacher gives you
  • See your assigned homework clearly in one place
  • Tick off homework as you complete it
  • Once all homework is done… unlock and play a Pokémon mini-game!

It's simple, motivating, and helps build good habits whether you're managing a classroom or doing your best as a pupil.
